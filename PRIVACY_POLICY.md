# Privacy Policy for D&D Player Chat

**Last Updated:** October 30, 2025

## Data Collection and Usage

D&D Player Chat is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension:

### What We Store
- **Webhook URL**: Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API to remember your chat webhook configuration.

### What We DON'T Collect
- We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers
- We do not track your usage or behavior
- We do not share any data with third parties
- All data stays on your local device

### Data Transmission
- Messages and dice rolls are sent directly from your browser to your configured webhook URL
- No data passes through our servers
- We have no access to the content you send

### Data Storage
- Your webhook URL is stored locally in your browser only
- You can delete this data at any time by removing the extension

### Third-Party Services
This extension sends data to webhook URLs that you configure. The privacy practices of those services are governed by their own privacy policies.
